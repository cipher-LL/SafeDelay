SafeDelay

Time-Locked Wallet for Bitcoin Cash

Lock your BCH in a smart contract vault. Funds are frozen until your chosen block height — no way to break the timelock, no matter what.

📦 UTXO Smart Contract 🔐 Single-Owner Security ⚡ No Third Parties 🦛 CashScript

How it works

1

Deploy a Vault

Create a SafeDelay contract with your public key and a lock duration (in blocks).

2

Deposit BCH

Send any amount of BCH to the contract address. It locks immediately on deposit.

3

Wait for Unlock

Funds are frozen until the lock end block. Add more anytime — the timer only extends.

4

Withdraw

After the lock expires, the owner can withdraw any amount directly to a P2PKH address.

Features

🔒 Hard Timelock

No premature withdrawals. The contract enforced lockEndBlock — even the owner can't bypass it.

📈 One-Way Extend

Owner can extend the lock to a later block. Once set, the lock can never be shortened.

↩️ Cancel Anytime

Owner can cancel and get a full refund at any time. Emergency access without losing funds.

🤝 Anyone Can Deposit

Third parties can deposit into your vault without needing your signature. Great for vesting.

📊 On-Chain Verified

Contract bytecode is publicly verifiable. No hidden rules — the code is the law.

⚡ CashScript / UTXO

Built on Bitcoin Cash UTXO model with CashScript. Different from EVM — more powerful and simpler.
